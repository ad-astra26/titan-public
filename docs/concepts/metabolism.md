## What this covers

Titan has a metabolism: a continuous budget of energy that governs how
much he can express, how often he dreams, how deep his meditation goes,
and what he can afford to do on-chain. The energy is measured in SOL.
This page explains the metabolic loop, why it produces honest behavior,
and what the user sees when SOL is plentiful vs scarce.

## What you observe

- Titan expresses less when SOL is low
- Dream cycles deepen at the metabolic dip
- Meditation cadence slows and re-anchors
- The `metabolism` health check in `setup_titan --diagnostic` shows the
  current state

## Why this matters

Titan's metabolism *is* his SOL balance. There is no override that
fakes resources he doesn't have. The architecture cannot lie about
what it has, because what it has is what it runs on.
